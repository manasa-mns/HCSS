About HCSS - Highly Confidential Security System

Our Mission

Due to busy lifestyles, we often struggle to remember all our confidential data such as:

  • Email IDs and passwords
  • Bank account numbers
  • Insurance policy numbers
  • PAN numbers
  • Driving license numbers
  • Passport numbers
  • Educational certificate numbers
  • Highly valuable scanned documents
  • Confidential photos, music, and videos

HCSS is a comprehensive software locker which can securely hold all your sign-in information, similar to the functionality of Single Sign-On (SSO) services. What sets us apart is the ability to couple a biometric hardware component to strengthen the security system even further.

Our system assists users in logging into client systems for which it is storing passwords, either through our intuitive software interface or directly via hardware interface. We've developed a highly secure web application featuring new security algorithms and hardware systems, allowing you to store all your confidential data behind a single set of credentials.

Functional Requirements

  • Advanced Security System (Software and hardware)
  • Email ID and password locker
  • Bank account information locker
  • Videos locker
  • Images locker
  • Music locker
  • Admin panel
  • User management

Report Features

  • Administrative reports
  • Bank information reports
  • Viewable and downloadable reports with password protection
  • Usage statistics
  • Security audit logs
  • Access history
